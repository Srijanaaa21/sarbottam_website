Sarbottam Cement Limited, founded in 2010 and operational since 2013, is Nepalâ€™s leading cement and clinker manufacturer with a capacity of 1 million tonnes of clinker and 2 crore cement bags annually. The company pioneered European-standard cement production in Nepal and introduced innovations like the Vertical Roller Mill and Cross Bar Cooler. With five limestone mines and its own quality lab, Sarbottam ensures a steady supply of top-grade raw materials. Employing over 500 people, its success is driven by continuous technological innovation in manufacturing.

Mr. Bishnu Prasad Neupane

Chairman

Mr. Bishnu Prasad Neupane is the Chairman of Saurabh Group and the whole time Managing Director of Sarbottam Cement Ltd. He has established himself as a successful self-made entrepreneur in manufacturing industries with more than 25 years of experience. Mr. Neupane is a pioneer in cement and steels industry in the country having extensive and in-depth business experience.

His involvement in business, qualification and experience is as follows:

  • Qualified as a Civil Engineer.
  • Extensive and in-depth business experience with major cement & steels.
  • Central Member of RECR Engineering Association.
  • Central Member of Swabalambi Samaj, IRL School, Nepal Heart Foundation.
  • Member of Nepal Clinker and Cement production organization.
