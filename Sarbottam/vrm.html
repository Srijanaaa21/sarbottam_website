VRM Technology

Vertical Roller Mill (VRM), can reduce up to 50% of energy consumption when compared to Ball Mills employed in cement grinding. In addition to the VRM, Sarbottam Cement worked closely with our equipment provider, FLSmidth, to implement the numerous innovations they have developed over the last decades.
